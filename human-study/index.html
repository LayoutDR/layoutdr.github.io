Project Information:

About the survey:

This survey will ask you to inspect three webpage layouts (images showing relevant portion of a webpage's layout). The first webpage layout is one that has a layout bug in the webpage. For example, a layout bug could be that some content of the page is partially or fully hidden from view. The webpage layout with the bug will be labelled Containing Bug. The second two layouts are of the same webpage but with the bug repaired and will be labelled as Repair A and Repair B. After inspecting all three webpage layouts, you must choose your preferred layout by choosing either the webpage containing the bug or one of the repaired webpage layouts. There is a total of ten sections with ten total questions containing three layouts each. In each section, you must read the short text-based description of the layout bug and identify it in the webpage before choosing your preferred webpage layout.

Please note:

  • Only one participation is allowed per person.
  • The duration of the study is expected to take about 10 to 15 minutes.
  • The webpages in the snapshots are related to software and will not contain explicit or distressing images.
  • You can withdraw from the study at any time before clicking on the final submit button.

What information is collected:

For the purpose of research and thereafter publication, we collect the following data points.

  • Your preference of layouts.
  • Non-personal data points reflecting your specific session.
    • An example is the total time spent in the study.

Questions:

If you have any questions, please send an email to questions@layoutdr.com.
